DirectDrop
Send files directly between your devices.
Works across your phone, tablet and computer — Windows, Mac, Android and iOS.

DirectDrop is a fast, private way to move photos, videos and files between your devices. Pair with a QR code or a 6-digit room code, approve the request, and your files go straight to the other device over a direct WebRTC connection.

Cross-platform

Transfer between phones, tablets and computers, regardless of platform.

Direct & private

File contents travel device-to-device over WebRTC and are never uploaded to our servers.

Quick pairing

Scan a QR code or enter a 6-digit room code to connect in seconds.

Multiple files

Send several files at once with batch approval and live transfer progress.

Reconnect

Reconnect to your saved devices, and get a notification even when the app is closed.

Transfer history

Keep track of the files you've sent and received.

Türkçe

DirectDrop, cihazlarınız arasında fotoğraf, video ve dosyaları hızlıca aktarmanızı sağlayan bir paylaşım uygulamasıdır. Telefon, tablet ve bilgisayarlarınız arasında doğrudan, platformdan bağımsız transfer. QR kod veya 6 haneli oda kodu ile eşleşin, isteği onaylayın ve dosyalarınızı doğrudan karşı cihaza gönderin. Dosya içeriği sunuculara yüklenmez; WebRTC ile doğrudan cihazdan cihaza iletilir.
