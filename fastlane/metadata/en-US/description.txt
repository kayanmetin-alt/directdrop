DirectDrop is a fast sharing app that lets you transfer photos, videos and files directly between your devices. Direct, platform-independent transfer between your phone, tablet and computer.

Pair with a QR code or a 6-digit room code, approve the connection request, and send your files straight to the other device.

Highlights:

• Cross-platform transfer: phone, tablet and computer
• Quick pairing with a QR code or room code
• Direct transfer over WebRTC
• File contents are sent without being uploaded to a server
• Send multiple files at once with batch approval
• Live transfer progress
• Progress indicator while preparing photos and videos
• Transfer history for sent and received files
• Reconnect to saved devices
• Connection request notifications even when the app is closed

DirectDrop uses Firebase-based signaling to set up pairing and connections. The content of transferred files is not stored on DirectDrop servers.

A simple, fast and reliable file transfer experience for everyday use.
